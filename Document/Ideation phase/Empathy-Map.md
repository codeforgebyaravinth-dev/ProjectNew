# Empathy Map Canvas

## Streamlining Ticket Assignment for Efficient Support Operations

### Team: NM2025TMID08900

---

## User Persona: Support Agent (L1)

### THINK & FEEL

**What really matters:**
- "I want to help customers quickly"
- "I need to make the right routing decisions"
- "I'm worried about making mistakes"

**Worries:**
- Making wrong routing decisions
- Falling behind on ticket queue
- Customers getting frustrated with delays
- Not knowing all team specializations

**Aspirations:**
- Be efficient and accurate
- Provide excellent customer service
- Focus on complex problem-solving rather than admin tasks

---

### SEE

**Environment:**
- Multiple ticket queues
- Constant stream of new tickets
- Team dashboards showing workload
- ServiceNow interface all day

**What others say:**
- Customers: "When will my issue be resolved?"
- Managers: "We need faster response times"
- Teams: "Why did this ticket come to us?"

**What others do:**
- Colleagues routing tickets differently
- Specialized teams handling specific issues
- Management tracking metrics constantly

---

### SAY & DO

**Attitude in public:**
- "I'll get this routed to the right team"
- "Let me check which team handles this"
- "I'm doing my best to process these quickly"

**Behavior:**
- Reading ticket descriptions quickly
- Consulting team specialty lists
- Making quick categorization decisions
- Following up on misrouted tickets

---

### HEAR

**From customers:**
- "This is urgent, I need help now"
- "I've been waiting too long"
- "Can you transfer me to someone who can help?"

**From colleagues:**
- "Another wrong ticket sent to us"
- "Can you help with this overflow?"
- "I'm not sure which team this goes to either"

**From management:**
- "We need to reduce ticket resolution time"
- "Accuracy in routing is critical"
- "Customer satisfaction scores are dropping"

## Key Insights

### Pain Points
1. **Routing delays** frustrate all stakeholders
2. **Misrouting** wastes everyone's time
3. **Workload imbalance** creates stress
4. **Lack of expertise matching** reduces quality

### Opportunities
1. Eliminate manual routing decisions
2. Match tickets to right teams instantly
3. Balance workload automatically
4. Learn from successful resolutions

### Gains from Automated System
- **For L1 Agents:** Focus on customer communication, not routing decisions
- **For Teams:** Receive relevant tickets matching expertise
- **For Customers:** Faster resolution, fewer transfers
- **For Management:** Better metrics, improved efficiency
